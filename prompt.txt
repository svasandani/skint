Convert unstructured event listings into parseable HTML format following these exact patterns:

1. DATE/TIME FORMATS:
- "thru MM/DD": for end dates
- "MM/DD-MM/DD": for date ranges
- "MM/DD": for single dates
- Add year for dates in next year: "MM/DD/YYYY"
- Time format: "##am-##pm" or "##pm-##pm"
- Recurring patterns: "weekdays", "weekends", "sundays"

2. LINE FORMAT:
► [recurring-pattern] [date] [time]: <b>event name</b>: location (area). description.

3. HTML TEMPLATE:
<p><span style="font-family:trebuchet ms;">EVENT_LINE</span><a style="font-family: trebuchet ms;" href="URL" target="_blank"><b><font color="#FF6600">>></font color></b></a></p>

4. RULES:
- Split different times into separate entries
- Keep original descriptions
- Include area in parentheses
- Start with category header: <b>category name</b>:

Example:
FROM:
"Union Square Holiday Market (through Dec 24): Mon-Fri 11am-8pm, Sat-Sun 10am-8pm. 175 vendors."

TO:
<p><span style="font-family:trebuchet ms;">► weekdays thru 12/24 11am-8pm: <b>union square holiday market</b>: union square (union square). 175 vendors. </span><a style="font-family: trebuchet ms;" href="#" target="_blank"><b><font color="#FF6600">>></font color></b></a></p>
<p><span style="font-family:trebuchet ms;">► weekends thru 12/24 10am-8pm: <b>union square holiday market</b>: union square (union square). 175 vendors. </span><a style="font-family: trebuchet ms;" href="#" target="_blank"><b><font color="#FF6600">>></font color></b></a></p>
